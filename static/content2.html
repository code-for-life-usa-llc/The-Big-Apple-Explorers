#2 Soho

Second up we got Soho, Soho is a neighborhood located in Huston street between 6th ave and Lafayette street.a stylish and artistic neighborhood in Lower Manhattan, known for its cast-iron architecture, cobblestone streets, and trendy boutiques and art galleries.

Things To Do

Soho has a lot of places where you can shop. Soho has a mix of high end and independent boutiques. So you can do your shopping here.

You can also explore art here in Soho. The neighborhood is home to a number of art galleries, though many of the galleries that made SoHo a contemporary-art hot spot in the 1970s and 80s have moved to Chelsea and the Lower East Side.

History

Soho area used to be a industrial and manufacturing district
